Honda CBR 300R
Mated to a six-speed transmission, the Honda CBR300R is powered by a 286cc, single-cylinder, liquid-cooled engine, producing 31PS of power at 8,500rpm and a peak torque of 27Nm at 7,250rpm. The fuel-injected motor is a bored out version of the 249cc engine seen on the CBR250R.
